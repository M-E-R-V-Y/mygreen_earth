KNOWING ALL ABOUT WASTAGES

To Understand the need of treating them

ALL THAT GOES INTO THE TRASH ?

Yes ! Your leftover meals, the bottle, the can, the
oily pizza box, the old notebook, the candy paper,
the broken toy, the worn out pair of sneakers ...
whatever you throw in the trash at home,
you obviously know that it is waste!

ALL THAT I TAKE TO RECYCLING CENTER

Your broken bike, the old mattress, the broken table,
the broken computer ... which does not necessarily
fit in the trash and that you take to the recycling center,
are also garbage. So that's all we throw away in our
daily and family life!

THE HISTORY OF WASTE

Companies also produce it in large quantities!

The office papers, the cooking oils from the
restaurant, the broken motor changed by the
mechanic, the wood chips from the furniture
manufacturer, the rubble from the demolition of a
building site, the cardboard boxes, wooden pallets,
unsold food from a supermarket… are also waste.


THE DEFINITION OF WASTE


Here is the definition of waste given by law: "Waste is any residue from a production, transformation or use process,
any substance, material, product ... that its holder intends to abandon".


Waste is therefore anything that is no longer useful to us and that we decide to throw away.


We constantly produce a multitude of waste!


To treat them better, the waste is classified by category according to its nature, its origin or its more or less toxic character.
